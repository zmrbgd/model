Key assumptions

Epidemology
72%
72% of all HCC cases are eligible for treatment with Y90.
Eligibility is based on the BCLC staging system and includes patients in early stage bridging to transplant, intermediate stage, and advanced stages with and without portal vein thrombosis
Y90 uptake
34%
34% of eligible patients would be treated with TheraSphere
The uptake of Y90 treatment in eligible patients is assumed to be progressive and specific to each HCC stage. On average, 34% of eligible patients would be treated with TheraSphere instead of the current standard of care per year
